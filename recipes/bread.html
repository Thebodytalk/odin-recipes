Absolutely Ultimate Potato Soup Cottage Cheese Blueberry Breakfast Cake

This homemade bread is one I've made personally. I followed a youtube video and a lot of adjustments I had made by feel so keep that in mind. We can call it a 'working' recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1.5 tablespoons of Active Dry Yeast
  • 8-10 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • Pinch or two of salt
  • 5.5 cups of all-purpose flour
